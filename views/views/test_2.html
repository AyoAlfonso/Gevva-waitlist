Daily Logs

A daily timeline of the child's activities. From naps to meals to medication. Parents know exactly where to pick up from.

Activity Calendars

Daily calendar of your scheduled work hours and childrens activities. Pull up the day and see what all your nanny kids have in one go. Also, get reminders when important dates are coming up.

Clock in/Clock out

Get a more accurate view of your work hours (especially the extra ones) with a GPS based clock in and clock out feature.

Emergency contact

All the childs emergency contacts stored in one place. From doctors, to poison control to ambulance.

Direct Messaging

Receive comments on posts and communicate directly with parents.

Photo/ Video Sharing

Share photos & videos with parents, so they never miss important moments.

Homework

Organise homework and projects. Never forget homework again. Attach every homework and project to due dates and receive reminders when due dates are coming up.

Multiple Families

Nanny for more than one family? No problem, you can add as many families as possible. All families are entirely separated. So data is never mixed up.
